Simon Says

Test your memory, concentration, and reaction speed by repeating the colour sequence.

How to Play

  • Watch carefully as the game flashes a colour sequence.
  • Remember the exact order.
  • Repeat the sequence by clicking the coloured buttons.
  • Every successful round adds one more colour.
  • Keep going as long as you can!
  • One wrong click ends the game.

Pro Tips

Stay focused while the pattern plays
Memorise groups, not individual colours
Take your time — there's no rush
Accuracy always beats speed
